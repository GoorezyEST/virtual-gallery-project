Magic Battle

Magic Battle is a rock-paper-scissors-based minigame. In this you can select between five different spells and then face another randomly generated one. It was a development that provided a lot of learning and left a feeling of satisfaction with its completion.

The main technology used in this project was the Angular framework. In order to better understand its concepts and functionalities.
